Vodacom Durban July

Vodacom Durban July is Africa's Greatest Horseracing Event, at Hollywoodbets Greyville Racecourse in Durban. The Vodacom Durban July draws heavily on horseracing‚Äôs deeply-rooted tradition of elegance and chic fashion.
