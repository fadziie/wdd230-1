Durban July

Vodacom Durban July is Africa's Greatest Horseracing Event, at Hollywoodbets Greyville Racecourse in Durban. The Vodacom Durban July draws heavily on horseracing’s deeply-rooted tradition of elegance and chic fashion. Being strongly associated with fashion means that The Durban July is not only a great opportunity for designers but also a fun spectator event - you'll get to rub shoulders with local celebrities and fashionistas. Comrades Marathon

The Comrades Marathon is the world’s oldest and largest ultramarathon run over a distance of approximately 90 km (56.1 miles) between the capital of the Kwazulu-Natal Province of South Africa, Pietermaritzburg, and the coastal city of Durban. Event Courtesy Of https://comrades.com

Sentinel Prime

A fig tree stands sentinel next to the Post Office tower clock, one of the oldest buildings in the Durban CBD. However, experts say the tree could cause considerable damage to the building which has been observing the hustle and bustle of life in the city centre for more than a century.
