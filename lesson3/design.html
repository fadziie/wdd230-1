Design Choices

Color Scheme

Our color scheme is inspired by the natural beauty of the Durban Coastal area. We chose a deep blue for the header and footer, representing the nearby sea, and a light green for the main content area, representing the greenery and trees. We also use a light gray for text to ensure readability.

Typography

We chose two complementary fonts for our website. Our headings use a bold sans-serif font to convey a modern, professional feel, while our body text uses a classic serif font for readability and elegance.

Examples

Here are some examples of how our color scheme and typography are applied throughout the site:

  • The header and footer have a blue background with white text.
  • The main content area has a light green background with white text.
  • We will use the following font family Helvetica Neue, sans-serif.
  • Headings use the sans-serif font in blue, while body text uses the serif font in white text.
  • Links are underlined and use the same blue color as headings.
