Our Target Audience

Our chamber of commerce serves local businesses and professionals in the Durban area.

Personas

Persona 1 - Small Business Owner

  • Name: Sarah
  • Age: 32
  • Occupation: Owner of a boutique clothing store
  • Goals: Increase foot traffic to her store, network with other business owners, learn about local events and resources

Persona 2 - Freelance Graphic Designer

  • Name: Alex
  • Age: 28
  • Occupation: Freelance graphic designer
  • Goals: Connect with potential clients, learn about local business events and opportunities, network with other professionals

Scenarios

  • Sarah needs to find out about upcoming business networking events in the Durban area.
  • Alex is looking for a local co-working space where he can meet with clients and work on his designs.
  • Sarah wants to promote a sale at her boutique store and is looking for affordable advertising options.
  • Alex needs to find a reliable printer to produce high-quality business cards for his freelance business.
  • Both Sarah and Alex are interested in attending a seminar on how to use social media to promote their businesses.

These scenarios help us understand some of the specific needs and goals of our target audience. By creating content and resources that address these scenarios, we can better serve our community of businesses and professionals.
